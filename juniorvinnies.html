Junior Vinnie's

What is Junior Vinnie's?

Junior Vinnie's is a Social Justice Action group for junior students at Marist College, to learn about and discuss social justice issues. Through this group, junior students arer able to learn about and support different social justice issues by creating informative posters, playing games, and having in depth conversations. This group helps prepare the junior students at Marist College for senior vinnies where they will be given the oppurtunity to go out and actually work with the Auckland Vinnie's team.

How does Junior Vinnie's have an impact on our society?

- help educate young people on important issues in our world/country

- help teach young people about the importance of service

- bring society together because we learn about the issues people in our society face

- creates a sense of unity

- inspires young people to be servant leaders
