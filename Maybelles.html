Maybelles

What is Maybelles?

Maybelles is Marist College's rest home choir. About once a term, we visit Everil Orr Rest Home and perform an arrangements of songs that we have prepared before hand.

What impact does Maybelle's have on our society?

The mission of Maybelle's is to give joy to the lederly. They are a very special part of our society and we want to give back to them. We also want to bring people of different experience together to serve and perform. We aim to make it a place where we can build on all different skill sets in order to allow everyone an oppurtunity to get involved.
